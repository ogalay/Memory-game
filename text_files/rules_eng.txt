Memory Game

Gameplay

2 modes to play
- Demo mode without openbci headset
- Real mode with openbci headset

Goal
Firstly the game is played with a deck of 20 cards, secondly with a desk of 36 cards.
In real mode eeg signal is collected for two games separately.
The cards are laid out in a grid face down.
The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.
The game ends when all cards have been correctly matched.
After the end of the second game the plot of eeg epochs difference is shown.
In demo mode the plot is made of synthetic data.
